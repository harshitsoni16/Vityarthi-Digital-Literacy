Stay Safe Online — Checklist (For College Students in India)
Prevention Tips

1)Use strong passwords for all accounts and don’t keep the same password everywhere.

2)Turn on two-factor authentication (OTP/login verification) for email, social media, and banking apps.

3)Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they say they are from the bank. (UPI Safety)

4)Always check the receiver’s name before making any UPI payment to avoid sending money to the wrong person. (UPI Safety)

5)Avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading files from random messages or emails.

6)Do not use public Wi-Fi for important work like payments or logging into bank accounts.

7)Keep your apps and phone updated to avoid security issues.

8)Be careful about what you post online and don’t share personal details like phone number, address, or ID.

If Something Goes Wrong
Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
Call the Helpline Number: 1930 (especially in case of financial fraud)
