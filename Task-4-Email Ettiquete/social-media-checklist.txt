Responsible Social Media Checklist

Do’s

1)Think before posting anything online
2)Keep your account private or controlled
3)Share only verified information
4)Use social media for learning and networking
5)Maintain a respectful tone in comments

Don’ts

1)Don’t share personal or sensitive details
2)Avoid posting offensive or harmful content
3)Don’t engage in unnecessary online arguments
4)Avoid oversharing your personal life
5)Don’t post anything that can affect your future
